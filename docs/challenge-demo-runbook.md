# Blaze Creator Intelligence Challenge Demo

## Demo Promise

Blaze Creator Intelligence tells a creator what changed, why it matters, and what to do next.

## Stable Flow

1. Open the connect screen and continue with Blaze.
2. Land on Dashboard with the real creator profile and compact channel status.
3. If offline, show the executive summary: last stream, last audience, last activity, last active time, and current direction.
4. Open Creator Intelligence from the compact dashboard card.
5. Walk through Audience, Content, Recommendations, and Opportunities.
6. Explain that audience confidence is shown beside the metrics it qualifies instead of overstating a trend.
7. Open Audience to explain viewer efficiency, conversion, engagement, momentum, and observation confidence.
8. Switch Growth between 7 days, 30 days, and all time.
9. Show follower, subscriber, viewer, Health Score, and activity history.
10. Open Notifications to show grouped, high-signal summaries.
11. Open Health Score and explain that v1 is an operating-health estimate while ecosystem research continues.
12. Open Settings to show benchmark observation history, distinct creators, and benchmark signal strength.

## Live Proof Points

- Blaze OAuth profile
- Live stream status and viewer count
- Real follower identity
- EventSub delivery through Socket.IO
- Neon snapshot persistence
- Decision insights generated from stored history
- Passive benchmark collection without changing Health Score v1
- Persisted benchmark research and collection status in Settings

## Demo Guardrails

- Keep the API, frontend, Neon, and EventSub connected before judging.
- Use a real stream or recent persisted history.
- Do not describe Health Score v1 as an ecosystem percentile.
- Describe benchmark confidence as research progress, not a score-quality percentage.
- Do not present a flat signal as growth; explain that more observations are required.
- Keep the dashboard concise and open the dedicated intelligence report for deeper analysis.
